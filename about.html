About Me

My name is David Mugisha, I am a senior Honors Physics Major, Mathematics minor from Fair Lawn, New Jersey. In my undergraduate studies, I have been privileged to join the College of Arts and Sciences Honors program which has brought about many opportunities for academic enrichment, research, and community building. After Undergraduate studies, I am planning to pursue my Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering for industry settings. Outside of school, I enjoy going to the movies, exploring food places, tennis, reading, and going to concerts. I have also found myself enjoying the activity of cooking lately. I also have a strong passion for research. Upon embarking in the COAS Honors Program, I have become a research assistant in Dr. Misra's Laser Spectroscopy laboratory, and it has been a privilege to work in the lab for over two years during my studies at Howard University.
